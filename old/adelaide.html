At Adelaide Apostolic Church, we welcome you just as you are, and want you to share in our joys and fellowship.We wish to assure you that you are precious to God and to us.So come join our family and make yourself at home.

Adelaide Apostolic Church is a Malayalam Independent mission-oriented church that believes in loving God and loving one another. Christ is the head of the church and we are part of His big family.

AAC comes under spiritual covering of TAMI (The Apostolic Ministries International). TAMI has more than 2000 mission fields and churches around the world
